Medication Therapy Management

According to American Pharmacist Association, Medication therapy management is defined as “distinct service or group of services that optimize therapeutic outcomes for individual patients”.

These services include but are not limited to

  • Selecting or changing therapy
  • Understanding and resolving medication related problems
  • Coordinating with your physician to provide the best possible health outcome
  • Documenting the interventional care and communicating with the PCPs regarding the important health care information
